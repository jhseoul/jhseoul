Company

Setting the standard,
building trust.

JH SEOUL connects products and brands, education and distribution, spaces and experiences —
expanding Korean value into the everyday life of new markets.

Who We Are

The standard that began in Seoul,
becoming a new choice for the world.

JH SEOUL doesn't stop at simply introducing Korean products abroad. We understand each market's environment and consumers' way of life, and design the products, brands, education, and spaces needed there together.

We source good ingredients and products ourselves, connect with local networks, and build every process required until a product truly finds its place in the market and in consumers' daily lives.

One Connected Business

Different businesses,
completing one market.

Product development connects to the medical and aesthetic field, education creates new professionals and consumers. Seoul Warehouse supplies curated products and brands, and Seoul Maeul delivers Korean beauty, food, café, and lifestyle as one experience.

None of JH SEOUL's businesses move in isolation. Products, education, medical, distribution, and space connect with one another to build a sustainable market foundation.

Product→Education→Medical→Distribution→Space

Vision

Seoul,
into everyday life around the world.

What we deliver isn't just a product. Our goal at JH SEOUL is to naturally connect Seoul's sensibility, Korea's standards, the attitude behind making good products, and a new way of living into everyday local life.

Until value that began in Korea becomes a trusted choice in new markets, including India, we will keep building the foundation needed, one step at a time.
